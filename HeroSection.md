Create a world-class cinematic intro experience for the Matisse Academy website. This intro should feel like entering an exclusive academy, not simply loading a website.

## Objective

Create a memorable first impression that communicates prestige, transformation, and excellence before revealing the homepage.

The experience should feel comparable in quality to luxury brands like Apple, Active Theory, and Loft Thirty One while remaining completely original.

Never copy layouts or animations directly.

---

## Overall Feeling

Premium

Elegant

Minimal

Confident

Luxurious

Timeless

Modern

No flashy gaming effects.

No excessive particles.

No unnecessary motion.

Every animation must have purpose.

---

## Background

The entire screen starts with a deep matte black background (#050505).

The background should feel infinite.

Use subtle cinematic lighting and soft ambient glow.

---

## Logo

Use the official Matisse Academy logo.

The logo must remain recognizable.

Transform it into a premium 3D object.

Materials:

• polished gold metallic border
• deep royal purple enamel
• realistic reflections
• beveled edges
• subtle imperfections like premium metal

Lighting should resemble a luxury watch advertisement.

The logo slowly breathes using an extremely subtle scale animation (around 1–2%).

Very gentle floating motion.

No spinning.

No bouncing.

---

## Camera

The camera starts close to the logo.

The logo occupies roughly 30–40% of the viewport.

Very shallow depth of field.

Soft cinematic bloom.

The camera remains almost still.

---

## User Interaction

Display only one instruction near the bottom:

↓

Scroll to Begin

Typography should be elegant and understated.

The animation should only begin once the user scrolls.

Never autoplay the transition.

---

## Scroll Transition

As the user scrolls:

The logo smoothly scales down.

The camera slowly pulls backward.

The logo moves naturally toward its final navigation position.

It should never disappear abruptly.

It transforms into the site's permanent logo.

The transition must feel physically believable.

Ease everything with premium GSAP animations.

---

## Reveal

While the logo transitions:

Fade in the hero background.

Fade in navigation.

Fade in typography.

Fade in the CTA.

Everything should appear progressively.

No hard cuts.

No jump transitions.

---

## Performance

Target 60 FPS.

Lazy load everything possible.

Optimize for desktop and mobile.

Respect prefers-reduced-motion.

---

## Technical Requirements

Use:

Next.js

TypeScript

TailwindCSS

GSAP

ScrollTrigger

Three.js or React Three Fiber only if necessary.

Structure the code cleanly with reusable components.

Keep animation values configurable.

Do not hardcode magic numbers.

The animation should be production-ready.
